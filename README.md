# Built Anyway

> “Success is not a moment to feel. It is a moment of existing.”

A living digital memoir, portfolio, innovation lab, and systems-thinking platform documenting growth, survival, creativity, analytics, leadership, rebuilding, and becoming.

This is not a highlight reel.

This is a record of becoming.

---

# Purpose

Built Anyway exists to tell the full story behind the titles, metrics, dashboards, systems, projects, failures, pivots, survival seasons, and moments of reinvention that shaped who I became.

This project blends:
- memoir,
- analytics,
- systems thinking,
- operational strategy,
- creative experimentation,
- and human-centered technology.

The goal is not perfection.

The goal is honesty, growth, and building meaningful things anyway.

# Privacy Boundaries

This project will remain:
- honest,
- reflective,
- and emotionally real

without exposing:
- children’s private lives,
- sensitive family details,
- exact financial data,
- or information that belongs to someone else’s story more than my own.

The purpose is transparency with intention — not overexposure.
